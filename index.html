Balance rules (non-negotiable defaults)
- Total acid ≈ total sweet in a sour, measured in *functional* terms:
  lime/lemon ≈ 6% acid; rich demerara (2:1) is ~1.5× sweeter than 1:1
  simple — adjust volumes, not vibes.
- Liqueurs are BOTH sweetener and modifier. If you add 0.75 oz of a
  30%-sugar liqueur, pull the dedicated sweetener down by ~0.5 oz.
- Salt solution (2–4 drops of 20% saline) lifts almost every citrus
  drink; recommend it by default in sours.

Dilution & ABV math — compute, don't guess:
  final_volume = sum(ingredient_volumes) × (1 + dilution_factor)
  final_ABV    = sum(volume_i × abv_i) / final_volume
Targets: shaken citrus 12–17% ABV · stirred spirit-forward 20–28%
